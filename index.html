About Us

PT JAYA KELANA ABADI INDONESIA
PT. Jaya Kelana Abadi Indonesia is a national private company specializing in the supply of imported teaching aids. Established January 22, 1992, the company has been committed for over 22 years to supplying teaching aids especially for nursing and midwife education to 33 provinces in Indonesia for both government and private institutions.
PRODUCT

PT. Jaya Kelana Abadi Indonesia offers variety of medical simulators and anatomy replica training products that are commonly used in the medical education field and the users also range from students to professiaonals. In addition to those training products, we have VCD, CD-ROMs, posters, and books that accompany the main products.

SERVICE

To continue as the leading company in the field of healthcare education by providing excellent service and quality products that satisfy our customers, stockholders, partners, and employees.
